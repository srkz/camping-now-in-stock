Membership is free.

Whether you’re an Extra with a loaded shack or you just found an old scanner in the garage, you’re welcome at BARC. This is a new club with a very limited roster currently, so get in on the ground floor and help kick start the club in your home town.

Who can join?

Anyone! You do need an FCC license to use the repeater, though.

Code of conduct

BARC follows the ARRL’s Amateur’s Code and a few simple additions:

  • Be considerate on the air and at meetings.
  • No harassment, slurs, or politics on the repeater.
  • Keep it family-friendly — assume kids are listening, because they are.
  • Help newcomers. You were one too, once.
  • If something goes wrong, tell a club officer. We’ll listen.
